# Refund Policy

## Refund window
Customers may request a refund within 30 days of the delivery date shown on the order.
Items must be unused, in original packaging, with all accessories and manuals included.
Refund requests made after 30 days are declined automatically unless the item arrived
defective or damaged, in which case the 30-day clock starts from the day the defect was
reported instead of the delivery date.

## Refund amount and method
Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days of
approval. Shipping charges are refunded only if the return is due to a VoltKart error
(wrong item shipped, defective on arrival, or damaged in transit). Customer-initiated
returns for reasons like "changed my mind" are refunded for the item price only.

## Auto-approval limits
Refunds under ₹5,000 for orders that are within the 30-day window and marked delivered
are auto-approved by the support agent without human review. Refunds between ₹5,000 and
₹8,000 require the order to have no prior refund on the same item. Refunds of ₹8,000 or
more always require escalation to a human agent, regardless of order status, because of
the higher fraud and cost exposure.

## Non-refundable situations
Gift cards, extended warranty add-ons after 15 days of purchase, and items marked
"final sale" in the product listing are not eligible for refund. Refund requests for
orders already marked "returned" are rejected as duplicates.
