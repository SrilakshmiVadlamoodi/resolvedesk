# Product FAQ — Wearables

## Battery life
VoltKart fitness bands last up to 10 days on a single charge with typical use (heart rate
tracking on, notifications on). Smartwatches last 2-3 days with always-on display, or up
to 7 days with the display set to raise-to-wake. Charging from empty takes about 2 hours
on the included magnetic charging dock.

## Water resistance and swimming
Wearables are rated 5 ATM, meaning they can be worn while swimming in a pool or shallow
open water, but are not rated for high-pressure water activities like diving or
water-skiing. Hot showers and saunas can degrade the seals over time and are discouraged.

## Health tracking accuracy
Heart rate, sleep, and step tracking are optical/accelerometer-based estimates intended
for general fitness awareness, not medical diagnosis. VoltKart wearables are not medical
devices and should not be used to make health decisions; customers with medical concerns
should be directed to consult a doctor.

## Syncing data
Wearables sync to the VoltKart Health app over Bluetooth automatically when the app is
open and the device is nearby. If sync stops working, unpairing and re-pairing the device
from the app's device settings usually resolves it.
