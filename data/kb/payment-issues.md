# Payment Issues

## Failed or double payment
If a customer reports being charged twice for the same order, or charged for an order
that shows as failed, the support agent should reassure the customer that duplicate or
failed-order charges are automatically detected by the payment processor and reversed
within 3-5 business days without any action needed. If more than 7 business days have
passed, escalate to a human agent with the transaction reference.

## Accepted payment methods
VoltKart accepts major credit and debit cards, UPI, and net banking. Cash on delivery is
available only for orders under ₹10,000 shipping to serviceable pin codes. Wallet-based
payments are not currently supported.

## Invoice and GST requests
Customers can request a copy of their invoice for any past order by order ID; invoices
include GST breakdown where applicable and are generated automatically, so the support
agent should treat this as an informational request rather than a policy decision.

## Refund not received
If a customer says an approved refund hasn't landed in their account after 7 business
days from approval, this should be escalated — it likely indicates a payment processor
issue that requires manual investigation, not something the standard refund flow can fix.
