# Returns Process

## Initiating a return
Once a refund is approved, the customer receives a prepaid return shipping label by
email. The item should be repackaged in its original box with all accessories and dropped
off at any courier partner location within 7 days of approval. Returns without original
packaging may be subject to a restocking deduction of up to 15% of the item price.

## Return inspection
Returned items are inspected at VoltKart's warehouse within 2-3 business days of arrival.
If the item fails inspection (missing accessories, signs of damage not disclosed at
return time), the refund can be reduced or denied, and the customer is notified with the
reason.

## Damaged-in-transit returns
If an item arrives damaged, the customer should report it within 48 hours of delivery
with photos. Damaged-in-transit reports skip the standard return flow — a replacement or
full refund (including shipping) is processed directly without needing the item shipped
back, since VoltKart's courier partner is liable for transit damage.
