# Shipping & Delivery

## Delivery timelines
Standard shipping delivers within 3-5 business days for metro areas and 5-8 business
days for non-metro areas. Express shipping, available at checkout for an added fee,
delivers within 1-2 business days in metro areas. Delivery timelines are estimates and
can shift during high-demand periods like festival sales.

## Tracking an order
Every order has a status of one of: placed, shipped, delivered, delayed, or returned.
Customers can ask the support agent for the current status and expected delivery date at
any time using their order ID. Once an order is marked "shipped", a courier tracking
number is attached and updates automatically as the courier scans the package.

## Delayed shipments
An order is marked "delayed" if it has not moved past its expected delivery window. For
delayed orders, the support agent should check the latest courier status and give the
customer a revised estimate. If a delayed order is more than 10 days past the original
estimate, the agent should offer to escalate for a replacement shipment or refund.

## Address changes
A shipping address can be updated by the support agent only while the order status is
still "placed" — once an order is "shipped", the courier has already picked it up and the
address cannot be changed through the system. In that case, the customer should be
directed to contact the courier directly, or the order can be flagged for redelivery
coordination after it is returned to VoltKart's warehouse.
