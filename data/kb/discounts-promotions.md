# Discounts & Promotions

## Applying a coupon
Coupon codes are entered at checkout in the "Promo code" field and apply before payment
is charged. Coupons cannot be applied retroactively to an order that has already been
placed, and the support agent cannot manually apply a missed coupon after checkout.

## Price-match and price-drop requests
VoltKart does not offer price matching against other retailers. If an item's price drops
within 7 days of a customer's purchase, the customer can request the price difference as
store credit; this is a manual review, not an automatic refund, so it should be
escalated with the order ID and the new price as evidence.

## Stacking promotions
Only one promo code can be applied per order. Sitewide sale discounts and promo codes
generally cannot be combined unless the promo explicitly says "stackable" in its terms.
