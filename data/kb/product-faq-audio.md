# Product FAQ — Audio

## Battery life and charging
VoltKart wireless earbuds and headphones typically run 24-30 hours on a full charge with
the charging case, and 6-8 hours per single charge for the earbuds alone. A full charge
from empty takes about 90 minutes over USB-C. Using third-party chargers rated above 10W
can degrade battery health faster and is not recommended.

## Pairing and connectivity
Audio products use Bluetooth 5.2 and support multipoint pairing with up to two devices at
once. If pairing fails, the customer should reset the device by holding the pairing
button for 10 seconds until the LED flashes, then reconnect from their device's Bluetooth
settings.

## Water and sweat resistance
Most VoltKart audio products carry an IPX4 rating, meaning they resist light splashes and
sweat during workouts but are not safe for swimming or submersion. Submersion damage is
not covered under warranty.
