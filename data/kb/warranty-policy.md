# Warranty Policy

## Standard warranty coverage
Every VoltKart electronics product ships with a manufacturer warranty covering
manufacturing defects and hardware failure under normal use. Coverage length depends on
the product category: audio products carry a 12-month warranty, smart home devices carry
an 18-month warranty, and wearables carry a 12-month warranty. The warranty period starts
on the delivery date, not the purchase date.

## What's covered
Warranty claims cover component failure (won't power on, battery no longer holds charge
below 50% of rated capacity, connectivity module failure) and manufacturing defects
(cracked casing from a factory defect, faulty buttons or ports). VoltKart will repair or
replace the unit at its discretion; if the exact model is discontinued, a comparable
model or store credit is offered instead.

## What's not covered
Physical damage from drops or liquid exposure, unauthorized repairs or modifications,
normal cosmetic wear, and damage from using non-VoltKart chargers or accessories are all
excluded from warranty coverage. Products purchased from unauthorized third-party
resellers are not eligible for VoltKart warranty service.

## Filing a claim
Warranty claims require the order ID and a description of the issue. Claims are logged
and routed to the warranty team; the support agent can open a claim but cannot approve
replacement shipments directly — that is a manual fulfillment step outside chat.
