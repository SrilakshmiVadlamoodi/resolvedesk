# Product FAQ — Smart Home

## Setup and app pairing
VoltKart smart plugs, bulbs, and cameras pair through the VoltKart Home app over Wi-Fi
(2.4GHz only — 5GHz networks are not supported). During setup, the device should be
within 3 meters of the router. If a device fails to appear in the app, restarting the
device and toggling Wi-Fi off/on on the phone usually resolves it.

## Compatibility with voice assistants
Smart home products integrate with both major voice assistant ecosystems once linked
through the VoltKart Home app's "Connected Services" menu. Any device added to a VoltKart
Home account automatically becomes available to linked voice assistants without
additional setup.

## Firmware updates
Firmware updates are pushed automatically when the device is on Wi-Fi and idle, typically
overnight. Customers can force a check by opening the device's settings in the app and
selecting "Check for updates".
